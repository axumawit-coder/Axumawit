Tic Tac Toe Rules

  1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  2. You are X, and your opponent is O.
  3. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  4. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  5. When all 9 squares are full, the game is a tie.
  6. To play, click on an empty square to place your mark.
  7. To reset the game, click on the Reset button.
  8. To exit the game, click on the Exit button.

Good luck!
